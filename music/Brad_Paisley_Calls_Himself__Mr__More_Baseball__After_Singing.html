Brad Paisley Calls Himself ‘Mr. More Baseball’ After Singing Before the World Series’ Longest Nights

Summary: Country star Brad Paisley performed the national anthem before both 18‑inning World Series games—the longest championship matchups in MLB history—and joked that he was now “Mr. More Baseball.”

Anthem Performance

During the 2025 World Series, Paisley sang the anthem at the 18‑inning opener and again before the 18‑inning finale, adding a country flair to the historic nights.

Game Context

The two 18‑inning contests tied the MLB record for longest World Series games, with the first lasting 4 hours 32 minutes and the second 4 hours 44 minutes.

Paisley's Baseball Persona

After the performances, the singer quipped that he’s “Mr. More Baseball” and highlighted his long‑standing love for the sport in post‑game interviews.

Fan and Media Reaction

Fans cheered his appearance, and sportswriters praised the added excitement of a celebrity anthem in a tense, historic series.
