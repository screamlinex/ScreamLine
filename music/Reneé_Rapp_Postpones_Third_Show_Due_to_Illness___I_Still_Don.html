Reneé Rapp Postpones Third Show Due to Illness: ‘I Still Don’t Have a Voice’

Summary: Singer Reneé Rapp postponed her third Charlotte show, rescheduling to November 7 to ensure a safe and fully prepared performance after ongoing illness.

Artist Update

Renee Rapp confirmed that she is still experiencing vocal issues and will not be able to perform as originally planned. The artist expressed gratitude for fans’ patience and emphasized the importance of her health for future shows.

Revised Performance Date

The Charlotte concert originally slated for November 2 has been moved to November 7 at the same venue. Rapp’s team assures that this change will allow her adequate recovery time.

Venue and Ticket Information

Tickets for the rescheduled show remain valid and can be accessed through the original purchase platform. Fans are advised to confirm seating and any additional entry requirements for the new date.

Health and Safety Measures

The venue will maintain current health protocols, including mask options and social distancing signage. Rapp’s team will monitor her vocal readiness closely to avoid further postponements.
