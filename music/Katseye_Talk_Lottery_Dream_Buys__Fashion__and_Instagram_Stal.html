Katseye Talk Lottery Dream Buys, Fashion, and Instagram Stalking on ‘Role Roulette’

Summary: In the latest installment of Rolling Stone’s Role Roulette, the girl group Katseye imagines how they’d navigate winning the lottery, going on blind dates, and setting the tone on Instagram, offering a glimpse into their personal dreams and public personas.

Lottery Wins

The members describe how they’d split a hypothetical $10 million jackpot, with half earmarked for charitable causes and the other half for personal investments. One member mentioned purchasing a beachfront villa, while another plans to fund a scholarship program for music students.

Blind Date Fantasies

Katseye outlines their ideal blind date scenarios: a rooftop dinner in Seoul, a private gallery tour, and a spontaneous road trip to Busan. They stress the importance of authenticity, stating that they’d prioritize mutual interests over social status.

Instagram Stalking

The group shares their strategies for monitoring fan engagement, noting that they’ll use analytics tools to track hashtag performance and audience sentiment. They also discuss curating a personal brand that balances candid moments with polished content.

Fashion Statements

In the Role Roulette segment, Katseye showcases their signature looks: a mix of avant‑garde streetwear and classic couture. They highlight upcoming collaborations with emerging designers and emphasize sustainability in their wardrobe choices.
