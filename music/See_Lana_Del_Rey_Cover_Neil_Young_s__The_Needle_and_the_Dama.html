See Lana Del Rey Cover Neil Young’s ‘The Needle and the Damage Done’ at Harvest Moon Concert

Summary: I thought we'd get started with a little bit of Neil," singer Lana Del Rey said before delivering a stirring rendition of Neil Young’s “The Needle and the Damage Done” during the Harvest Moon charity concert. The performance, part of a lineup that highlighted the album’s legacy, was met with enthusiastic applause and highlighted the event’s fundraising success.

Concert Overview

The Harvest Moon concert, held at the historic Avalon Theatre, drew over 1,200 attendees for a benefit supporting mental health initiatives. The program featured a mix of contemporary artists and classic covers, culminating in Del Rey’s unexpected homage to Neil Young.

Lana Del Rey’s Performance

Del Rey opened with a brief introduction, citing Young’s influence on her music. She then performed “The Needle and the Damage Done,” delivering the emotionally charged lyrics with a hushed, intimate arrangement that honored the original while adding her signature cinematic style.

Audience Reaction

The crowd responded with a standing ovation, and many attendees posted live reactions on social media. Critics noted the performance’s authenticity and its impact on the event’s overall atmosphere.

Charity Impact

Funds raised at the concert exceeded last year’s total by 25%, thanks in part to the draw of Del Rey’s cover. All proceeds will be allocated to the Harvest Moon Foundation’s programs for young adults coping with substance abuse and mental health challenges.
