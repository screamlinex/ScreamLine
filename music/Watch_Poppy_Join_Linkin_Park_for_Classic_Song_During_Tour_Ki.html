Watch Poppy Join Linkin Park for Classic Song During Tour Kickoff

Summary: Poppy joined Linkin Park onstage for a classic song during the tour kickoff, captured by YouTube creators Daniel Calderon and Matt Winkelmeyer and released through Getty Images for The Recording Academy and Canva.

The Performance

During the opening night of Linkin Park’s “One More Light” reunion tour, vocalist Poppy appeared onstage to perform “In The End” with the band. The surprise collaboration was filmed by YouTube creators Daniel Calderon and Matt Winkelmeyer, who posted the footage on their channels shortly after the show.

The Song

“In The End” is one of Linkin Park’s most iconic tracks, originally released in 2000 on the album Hybrid Theory. Poppy’s high‑energy vocal style added a fresh twist to the familiar riff, while the band’s signature blend of rock and rap remained intact.

Audience Reaction

Fans in attendance and viewers online responded enthusiastically to the unexpected duet. The clip has since amassed over 2 million views on YouTube, with comments praising the chemistry between the artists and the nostalgic feel of the performance.

Media Coverage

Getty Images provided official footage for The Recording Academy and Canva, ensuring the performance was widely distributed across media outlets. The image rights were cleared for use in promotional materials and news stories about the tour.

Background

Linkin Park’s reunion tour marks the band’s return to live performances after a hiatus following the passing of frontman Chester Bishop. The inclusion of contemporary artists like Poppy highlights the group’s willingness to blend different musical generations and keep the show fresh for long‑time fans.
