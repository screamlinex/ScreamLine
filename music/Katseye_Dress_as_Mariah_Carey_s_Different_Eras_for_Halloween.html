Katseye Dress as Mariah Carey’s Different Eras for Halloween (and She Approves!)

Summary: The girl group performed “Gabriela” at the Halloween event, styling each member in costumes that pay tribute to Mariah Carey’s iconic eras, with Bowen Yang as a surprise guest. The outfits ranged from the 1990s “Dreamlover” look to the 2000s “Rainbow” vibe, earning enthusiastic applause from the crowd.

Event Highlights

The concert took place at the downtown amphitheater on October 31st, featuring a mix of dance choreography and live vocals. The group’s rendition of “Gabriela” was punctuated by synchronized lighting that mirrored Mariah’s signature stage setups from the 1990s.

Dress Details

Each member wore a carefully selected ensemble reflecting a specific Mariah era: a glittering “Dreamlover” jumpsuit, a neon “Honey” crop top, a 2000s “Rainbow” feathered dress, and a 2010s “Merry Christmas” sequined jacket. Accessories such as oversized sunglasses, feather boas, and signature high-heel boots completed the looks.

Mariah Era Breakdown

The costumes highlighted Mariah’s evolution: the 1990s “Dreamlover” era with its polished, ball‑gown aesthetic; the early 2000s “Rainbow” era featuring bright, theatrical makeup and bold jewelry; and the later 2010s “Merry Christmas” era known for its glitter and festive motifs.

Bowen Yang Appearance

Comedy actor Bowen Yang joined the group on stage wearing a playful “Mariah”‑inspired ensemble, complete with a faux microphone and a stylized “Said It’s My Life” hat. His cameo added a humorous twist to the tribute performance.
