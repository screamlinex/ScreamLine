Kelly Osbourne’s Son Recreates Grandpa Ozzy’s Wild Bat Incident in Adorable Video

Summary: “Learned from the greatest, Papa!” Kelly Osbourne wrote after her son Sidney, two, mimicked Ozzy Osbourne’s infamous bat encounter in a short, adorable video that is already trending on social media.

Background of the Bat Incident

In 1982, during a concert in San Bernardino, California, Ozzy Osbourne famously threw a live bat onto the stage, prompting a chaotic scene. The clip, widely circulated on VHS and later online, became one of the most recognizable moments in rock history.

Sidney’s Adorable Recreation

Filmed on TikTok and reposted on Instagram, the two‑year‑old donned a mock “rockstar” outfit and mimicked the bat gesture, complete with a playful gasp. The clip, shot in the Osbourne family’s backyard, lasts 17 seconds and has already amassed over 3 million views.

Kelly Osbourne’s Reaction

Kelly posted the video with the caption, “Learned from the greatest, Papa!” She added that Sidney “just had to do it” and that the moment reminded her of the family’s shared sense of humor.

Public Response

Fans of both the Osbournes and classic rock have shared the clip across Twitter, Reddit, and fan forums. Many praised the wholesome family dynamic, while others expressed nostalgia for the original bat incident.

Behind the Scenes

Sources say the family recorded the video as a lighthearted family activity, with no intention of going viral. However, the clip quickly spread after Kelly’s initial post, demonstrating the power of celebrity-driven content on social media.
