Justin Bieber Attends World Series in Blue Jays Jersey, Gives Dodger’s Shohei Ohtani the Thumbs Down

Summary: Canadian pop star Justin Bieber attended Game 3 of the World Series, wearing a Toronto Blue Jays jersey. While cheering for the Jays, he made a playful gesture toward Dodgers star Shohei Ohtani, giving him a thumbs‑down on the field.

Game 3 Recap

The Toronto Blue Jays faced the Los Angeles Dodgers in a pivotal third game of the 2025 World Series. With the series tied 1‑1, the Jays won 5‑3, taking a 2‑1 lead. The win came on a late rally, with a home run from Miguel Díaz in the ninth inning. The Dodgers’ pitcher, Shohei Ohtani, struggled with two walks and a hit, limiting his impact in the game.

Bieber’s Blue Jays Appearance

Justin Bieber arrived at Rogers Centre wearing a blue Jays cap and a full‑length jersey. He was photographed by the stadium camera crew and was seen interacting with fans in the front row. The singer’s presence drew media attention and increased social media buzz around the game.

Thumbs‑Down Gesture Toward Ohtani

During the intermission, Bieber was spotted standing beside a dugout seat as the Dodgers’ Ohtani walked onto the field. He raised his hand in a brief thumbs‑down gesture, which was captured by the television broadcast. Ohtani responded with a smile, and the moment was replayed across sports networks for its humorous contrast to the competitive stakes.

Fan and Media Reaction

Blue Jays supporters celebrated Bieber’s appearance, posting photos on Twitter and Instagram with the hashtag #BieberBlueJays. Dodgers fans expressed amusement and some disappointment at the gesture. Sports commentators noted the incident as a light‑hearted distraction from the intense playoff competition.
