Selena Gomez Gives Us a ‘Fun, Edgy’ Look at Her ‘In the Dark’ Video for ‘Nobody Wants This’ Season 2 Song

Summary: "Selena's voice has this aching vulnerability that made the scene feel even more raw," show creator Erin Foster told Rolling Stone after the release of Selena Gomez’s new music video for the *Nobody Wants This* Season 2 track *In the Dark*.

Video Concept and Narrative

The clip opens with Gomez standing alone in a dimly lit, industrial set that mirrors the show’s gritty aesthetic. She moves through a series of stark, angular rooms, each representing a different emotional state tied to the song’s lyrics. The narrative follows a simple yet effective progression from isolation to confrontation, culminating in a dramatic, synchronized dance sequence.

Visual Style and Direction

Directed by renowned visual artist Liza Morales, the video uses high-contrast lighting and muted color palettes to emphasize the song’s moody atmosphere. Quick cuts, close‑up shots of Gomez’s facial expressions, and occasional slow‑motion moments highlight her vocal intensity and enhance the overall cinematic quality.

Performance Highlights

Selena Gomez delivers a powerful vocal performance that showcases her range from breathy whispers to soaring choruses. Her choreography—performed with a small, tight crew—adds an energetic edge that balances the video’s darker themes. The synergy between the music and movement reinforces the track’s emotional impact.

Production and Reception

Produced by the *Nobody Wants This* production team, the video was shot over two days in Los Angeles. Early reviews on social media praise Gomez’s “edgy” look and the authentic feel of the visuals. The clip has already amassed over 12 million views on YouTube within its first week, indicating strong audience engagement.
