Miguel Will Unleash ‘Caos’ on 2026 Tour

Summary: Miguel will embark on his 2026 "Caos" tour, bringing singer‑songwriter Jean Dawson on the road for all North American dates.

Tour Overview

Miguel’s “Caos” tour kicks off on February 12, 2026, in Seattle and wraps up on June 30, 2026, in Los Angeles. The tour spans 45 shows across the United States and Canada, featuring a mix of intimate venues and large arenas.

The setlist focuses on tracks from the new album “Caos,” alongside classic hits such as “Come Through” and “Sure Thing.”

Jean Dawson’s Participation

Jean Dawson will serve as a co‑lead vocalist and percussionist for the North American leg. Her dynamic stage presence complements Miguel’s soulful performances, adding a fresh vocal harmony to songs like “Tough Love” and “Me, You & the Kids.”

Dawson’s inclusion marks a first for Miguel, who has previously toured with guest artists such as Beyoncé and J. Cole.

Ticketing & Pricing

Tickets for the North American shows are available through Ticketmaster, with prices ranging from $45 to $280 depending on venue and seat location. Early‑bird specials end on January 15, 2026.

VIP packages, which include a meet‑and‑greet, exclusive merchandise, and a signed poster, start at $350.

Production Highlights

The tour’s production features a high‑definition LED backdrop, a full‑band orchestra, and interactive lighting designed to amplify the emotional intensity of each track.

Environmental initiatives include biodegradable confetti and a partnership with local waste‑reduction programs at each venue.

Additional Information

All dates are subject to change. Fans are encouraged to monitor the official Miguel website and social media channels for updates, refunds, or schedule adjustments.

Health and safety protocols will adhere to venue guidelines, with masks optional and hand sanitizing stations provided throughout the stage area.
