Watch Steve Martin, Alison Brown Perform Instrumental Track ‘Let’s Get Out of Here’ on ‘Fallon’

Summary: Steve Martin and Alison Brown performed their instrumental track “Let’s Get Out of Here” on NBC’s “The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon”. The duo, known for their collaborative bluegrass album “Safe, Sensible and Sane,” showcased the song’s blend of banjo, mandolin, and jazz influences.

Live Performance on Fallon

During a Saturday night taping, Martin and Brown delivered a high‑energy rendition of “Let’s Get Out of Here” featuring Martin’s banjo and Brown’s mandolin. The performance was praised for its tight harmonies and improvisational flair.

Background of the Collaboration

Steve Martin, a renowned comedian and banjo player, and Alison Brown, a celebrated mandolinist, have worked together since 2018. Their partnership culminated in the 2023 album “Safe, Sensible and Sane,” which blends bluegrass with jazz and world‑music elements.

Album Overview

“Safe, Sensible and Sane” features 12 tracks, including “Let’s Get Out of Here.” The album received positive reviews for its inventive arrangements and cross‑genre appeal.

Audience and Critical Reception

Critics highlighted the duo’s ability to fuse humor and musicianship. Fans responded enthusiastically on social media, with the Fallon performance trending shortly after airing.
