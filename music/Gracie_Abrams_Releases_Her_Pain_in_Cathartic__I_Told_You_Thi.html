Gracie Abrams Releases Her Pain in Cathartic ‘I Told You Things’ Live Performance From Red Rocks

Summary: Gracie Abrams premiered her emotionally charged single “I Told You Things” in a live performance at Red Rocks Amphitheatre, recorded for the Apple Music Live concert special now available exclusively on Apple Music.

Red Rocks Sets the Tone

On a crisp evening in Denver, the iconic Red Rocks Amphitheatre provided a resonant backdrop for Abrams’s set. The natural acoustics amplified the intimate arrangement, allowing her voice and piano to echo across the sandstone walls.

Song Highlights

“I Told You Things” blends stripped‑down piano with subtle string layers, underscoring the raw vulnerability of its lyrics. Abrams’s performance emphasized the song’s themes of regret and catharsis, delivering a palpable emotional impact.

Apple Music Live Release

The concert special, titled “Apple Music Live: Gracie Abrams,” premiered on October 29, 2024, and is available only on the Apple Music streaming platform. The 45‑minute feature includes the full Red Rocks set, backstage footage, and a brief interview with Abrams.

Artist’s Reflections

In an interview with the Apple Music editorial team, Abrams said, “Playing the song live in front of a crowd that felt the same pain was the most cathartic experience I’ve had. It’s a reminder that we’re not alone in our struggles.”
