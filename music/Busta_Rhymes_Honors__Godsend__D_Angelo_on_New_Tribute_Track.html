Busta Rhymes Honors ‘Godsend’ D’Angelo on New Tribute Track ‘Magic’

Summary: Over the Voodoo track “One Mo'Gin,” Busta Rhymes recalls his first meeting with the late R&B icon D’Angelo and celebrates his musical legacy on a new tribute song titled “Magic.”

New Track “Magic” Drops on Upcoming Release

Busta Rhymes’ forthcoming album, set for release in late 2025, includes the single “Magic,” a homage to the late D’Angelo. The track was unveiled during a live stream on Busta’s official YouTube channel, where he premiered the song and shared the inspiration behind it.

Busta’s Personal Connection to D’Angelo

In a brief interview with Rap Radar, Busta recounted meeting D’Angelo at a 2003 music conference in New York. “It was my first time hearing him live, and I was blown away by his voice and the depth of his songwriting,” Busta said. The encounter left a lasting impression, prompting him to honor the late artist decades later.

Musical Composition and Production

“Magic” blends Busta’s signature rapid-fire verses with a smooth, neo‑soul chorus that echoes D’Angelo’s vocal style. Produced by industry veteran Nineteen85, the track incorporates a subtle piano loop and a laid‑back drum groove reminiscent of D’Angelo’s “Untitled (How Does It Feel).” The lyrical content references themes of love, spirituality, and artistic integrity.

Reception and Cultural Significance

Early listeners on streaming platforms have praised the track for its respectful nod to D’Angelo’s influence on contemporary R&B. Music critics note that Busta’s tribute serves to introduce D’Angelo’s music to a new generation of hip‑hop fans, reinforcing the late artist’s enduring legacy.
