Zohran Mamdani Shares Message to Vote at PinkPantheress’ Tour Opener: ‘Our Time Has Come’

Summary: At PinkPantheress’ tour opener, New York City mayoral candidate Zohran Mamdani urged fans to vote while performing “True Romance.” He thanked the singer for her support and delivered a concise message encouraging civic participation.

Event Details

The concert took place at Madison Square Garden on Monday, September 23, with PinkPantheress opening for the headline act. The venue’s 20,000‑seat capacity was filled to near capacity, creating a vibrant atmosphere for the first show of the artist’s North American tour.

Mamdani’s Message

During a brief intermission, Mamdani stepped onto the stage to address the crowd. He expressed gratitude for the support he has received from the local community and urged attendees to cast their ballots in the upcoming mayoral election. “Our time has come,” he said, “and the only way to shape our city’s future is through the ballot box.”

Audience Response

The crowd responded enthusiastically, with many fans chanting “Vote!” and raising their phones to record the moment. Social media posts from attendees highlighted the unexpected political moment, with the hashtag #VoteWithMamdani trending for a short time.

Political Implications

Mamdani’s appearance at a popular music event underscores his strategy of engaging younger voters. Analysts note that the campaign’s focus on civic education and voter turnout aligns with the demographic that attends contemporary pop concerts. The gesture is expected to boost visibility among a key voting bloc ahead of the election.
