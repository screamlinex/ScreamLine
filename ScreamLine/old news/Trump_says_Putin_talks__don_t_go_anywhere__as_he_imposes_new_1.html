Global News / October 23, 2025

Trump says Putin talks 'don't go anywhere' as he imposes new sanctions

Summary: The US has announced new sanctions on Rosneft and Lukoil, Russia's two largest oil companies, over continued fighting in Ukraine.

Trump’s Bold Statement to Putin

In a televised address, President Donald Trump told Russian President Vladimir Putin that the U.S. would not back down. “Don’t go anywhere,” Trump warned, underscoring the administration’s resolve amid the ongoing Ukraine conflict.

Details of the New Sanctions

The sanctions target the export of equipment, technology, and services to Rosneft and Lukoil, effectively choking the companies’ access to the global market. Assets held by the firms in the United States will be frozen, and U.S. entities are prohibited from providing them with any financial or logistical support.

Global Reactions and Implications

European allies have expressed support for the sanctions, while Russia has called the move “unnecessary escalation.” The U.S. Treasury Department stated that the measures aim to deter further aggression in Ukraine and signal a unified front among Western nations.

Energy Market Fallout

Analysts predict a short-term spike in oil prices as supply chains adjust to the new restrictions. However, the long‑term impact on global energy markets remains uncertain, with many firms scrambling to diversify their sources.
