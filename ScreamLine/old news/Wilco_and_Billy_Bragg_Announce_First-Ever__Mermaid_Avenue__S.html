Wilco and Billy Bragg Announce First-Ever ‘Mermaid Avenue’ Show

Summary: The band and the U.K. folk singer will team up to play songs from their collaboration with Woody Guthrie's estate at next year's Solid Sound Festival.

Official Announcement

Wilco and Billy Bragg confirmed today that they will perform a live rendition of their “Mermaid Avenue” project at the upcoming Solid Sound Festival in 2025. The announcement was made via a joint statement on both artists’ official websites.

Mermaid Avenue Legacy

The original “Mermaid Avenue” recordings were released in 1998 and 2002, pairing Woody Guthrie’s unpublished lyrics with contemporary songwriting by Wilco and Bragg. The albums received critical acclaim for their blend of folk tradition and modern sensibility.

Event Details

The performance is scheduled for July 15, 2025, at the Greenfield Pavilion, part of the Solid Sound Festival lineup. Tickets will go on sale next month, with a special VIP package that includes a meet-and-greet with the artists.

Reactions and Expectations

Music critics have praised the duo’s decision to bring the project to the stage, noting that it offers a rare opportunity to experience the songs live. Fans of both Wilco and Bragg are expected to fill the venue, as the collaboration has maintained a dedicated following since its release.
