Nicole Kidman, Gwyneth Paltrow, Ayo Edebiri and Dakota Johnson to Attend Vogue World: Hollywood

Summary: The highly anticipated Vogue World: Hollywood event on October 26 at Paramount Studios is shaping up to be a star‑powered spectacle. Nicole Kidman, Gwyneth Paltrow, Ayo Edebiri, Dakota Johnson, and Miley Cyrus headline an already impressive guest list that also includes Ashley Park, Greta Lee, Karol G, Maitreyi Ramakrishnan, Mona Zaki and singer Rina Sawayama.

Event Overview

Vogue World: Hollywood is the brand’s flagship showcase of Hollywood’s most daring fashion, blending cinematic flair with runway innovation. Set against the iconic backdrop of Paramount Studios, the event will feature a curated lineup of designers who reinterpret the Hollywood aesthetic for the modern era. Attendees can expect an immersive experience that blurs the lines between film and fashion.

Star‑Studded Guest List

Beyond the headline names, the event’s social calendar is brimming with A‑list talent. Nicole Kidman’s elegant presence, Gwyneth Paltrow’s understated chic, and Dakota Johnson’s playful edge are sure to set the tone. Ayo Edebiri’s vibrant energy and Miley Cyrus’s bold style will add dynamic flair, while the inclusion of rising stars like Maitreyi Ramakrishnan and Mona Zaki signals Vogue’s commitment to fresh talent.

Fashion Highlights & Predictions

Designers are poised to push boundaries, with expectations of avant‑garde silhouettes, metallic accents, and a resurgence of 90s-inspired silhouettes. Attendees will be treated to a showcase that fuses high‑fashion couture with street‑style sensibilities, reflecting Hollywood’s ever‑evolving cultural landscape.

Behind the Scenes

While the runway steals the spotlight, the backstage buzz promises equally compelling moments. From whispered collaborations between designers and the creative teams of the featured celebrities, to the meticulous prep that ensures each look is runway‑ready, the event offers a rare glimpse into the meticulous artistry behind Hollywood fashion.
