Ariana Grande Remembers How Madonna Gave Her a ‘Warm Welcome’ When She Got Famous

Summary: Ariana Grande recalled Madonna’s welcoming gesture when she first entered the music scene, while also highlighting Mariah Carey and Beyoncé as early supporters who helped launch her career.

Madonna’s Warm Welcome

During a recent interview, Grande spoke about Madonna’s reaction to her debut single. “When I first came out, Madonna sent me a card saying she was excited to see what I’d do,” she said. “Her encouragement meant a lot because I was just starting out.” The pop icon’s supportive message helped Grande feel validated in a competitive industry.

Mariah Carey’s Support

Grande also reflected on Mariah Carey’s early endorsement. “Mariah reached out to me after hearing my first demo and told me I had a voice like hers,” she explained. “Her belief in my talent gave me confidence during the first few months of touring.” Carey’s mentorship is credited with helping Grande refine her vocal technique.

Beyoncé’s Early Endorsement

In addition to Madonna and Carey, Beyoncé’s support was highlighted. “Beyoncé invited me to perform at a small club in New York and praised my stage presence,” Grande noted. “That exposure helped me build a fan base and get noticed by record labels.” Beyoncé’s backing is seen as a pivotal moment in Grande’s ascent.

Ariana’s Reflections

Grandé emphasized the importance of early encouragement from established artists. “Having those big names say ‘I believe in you’ made all the difference,” she said. “I’m grateful for their support and hope to pay it forward to the next generation of musicians.”
