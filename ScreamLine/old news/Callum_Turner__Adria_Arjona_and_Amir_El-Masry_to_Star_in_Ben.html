Callum Turner, Adria Arjona and Amir El-Masry to Star in Ben Sharrock’s ‘Alone Together,’ Florian Zeller Producing

Summary: Callum Turner (The Boys in the Boat, Masters of the Air) and Adria Arjona (Hit Man, Splitsville) headline Ben Sharrock’s next feature, “Alone Together,” alongside Amir El‑Masry (Giant, 100 Nights of Hero). The project is produced by Florian Zeller and promises a blend of sharp drama and intimate character work.

Casting Highlights

Turner brings a seasoned intensity to his role, having recently earned acclaim for his nuanced performance in “Masters of the Air.” Arjona, known for her versatility in both action and drama, is poised to deliver a compelling presence that balances vulnerability with fierce determination. El‑Masry, whose breakout role in “Giant” showcased his dynamic range, adds depth to the ensemble as a character grappling with identity and ambition.

Director and Production

Ben Sharrock, who first captivated audiences with his dark comedy “Limbo,” returns to the director’s chair with a more grounded, character‑driven narrative. He’s partnered with producer Florian Zeller, celebrated for his work on “The Father” and “The White Tiger,” to bring a fresh, emotionally resonant vision to the screen.

Plot Overview

“Alone Together” follows three strangers who find themselves unexpectedly bound by circumstance. As they navigate personal crises and intersecting paths, the film explores themes of connection, resilience, and the human need for belonging in an increasingly fragmented world.

Production Details

Filming began in late 2024 across locations in London and Prague, with a modest budget that emphasizes strong performances over spectacle. The production team has highlighted the film’s commitment to authentic storytelling, employing natural lighting and intimate set designs to heighten the emotional stakes.
