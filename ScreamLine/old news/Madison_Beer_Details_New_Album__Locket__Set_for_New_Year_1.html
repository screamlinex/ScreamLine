Madison Beer Details New Album ‘Locket’ Set for New Year

Summary: “After writing the album, it feels like each song lives within this metaphorical locket for safe keeping,” singer says of upcoming LP

Album Overview

Madison Beer announced her new studio album, “Locket,” which is set to drop on January 18, 2025. The project marks her first full-length release since 2021 and is described as a blend of pop, R&B, and electronic influences.

Track Highlights

The 13-track album opens with the upbeat single “Golden Hour,” followed by the introspective “Shadows,” and closes with the emotionally charged “Echoes.” Several songs feature collaborations with producers known for their work in contemporary pop.

Creative Process

Beer revealed that the album’s concept revolves around the idea of a locket as a safe space for personal memories. She spent months writing and refining lyrics that reflect her journey through love, loss, and self-discovery.

Release and Promotion

“Locket” will be available on all major streaming platforms, with a physical edition including a limited-edition locket-shaped CD. Beer will perform select tracks on a live-streamed concert on the release day and has scheduled appearances on several radio shows in the weeks ahead.
