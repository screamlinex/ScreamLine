Foo Fighters Ramp Up the Rock on New Song 'Asking for a Friend'

Summary: Jamie McCarthy, Getty Images / Roswell

New Release

The Foo Fighters unveiled their latest single, "Asking for a Friend," on September 12, 2024. The track is the lead single from their forthcoming tenth studio album, slated for release in early 2025.

"Asking for a Friend" blends the band's signature hardâ€‘rock riffing with a soaring vocal hook. The lyrics explore themes of solidarity and personal struggle, delivered over a driving drum groove and layered guitar work.

Band's Response

Frontman Dave Grohl said, "We wanted to create a track that feels both urgent and hopeful. The band tested it live at Roswell and the audience response was electric." Guitarist Chris Shiflett noted the song's structure as a nod to the band's early 2000s sound.

Critical Reception

Early reviews from Rolling Stone and NME praise the single for its energetic production and relatable lyrics. Critics highlight the track as a strong indicator of the album's direction and the band's continued evolution.
