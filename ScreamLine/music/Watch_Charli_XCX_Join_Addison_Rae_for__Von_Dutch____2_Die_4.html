Watch Charli XCX Join Addison Rae for ‘Von Dutch,’ ‘2 Die 4’ at Los Angeles Show

Summary: The pop stars, who have been collaborating since 2023, teamed up on stage at the Greek Theatre in Los Angeles for a surprise set featuring Charli XCX’s “Von Dutch” and “2 Die 4” alongside Addison Rae’s new releases.

Performance Highlights

Charli XCX joined Addison Rae onstage at the Greek Theatre, performing “Von Dutch” and “2 Die 4” from her upcoming album. The duo delivered a high‑energy set that blended pop and electronic beats.

Collaboration History

Since 2023, the artists have collaborated on several tracks, including “Love Is A Weapon” and “Starlight.” This live appearance marks their first joint performance in front of a full audience.

Audience Reaction

Attendees praised the chemistry between the performers, with many social‑media posts highlighting the seamless transition between their styles.

Upcoming Projects

Charli XCX is set to release her new album later this year, while Addison Rae plans to expand her music catalog with a forthcoming EP.
