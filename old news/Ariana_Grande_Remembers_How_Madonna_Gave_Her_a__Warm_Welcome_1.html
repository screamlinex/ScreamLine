Ariana Grande Remembers How Madonna Gave Her a ‘Warm Welcome’ When She Got Famous

Summary: Ariana Grande recalled Madonna’s warm welcome when she first rose to fame, while also acknowledging early support from Mariah Carey and Beyoncé.

Madonna's Warm Welcome

During a backstage encounter at a 2012 pop festival, Ariana Grande was greeted by Madonna, who praised her vocal talent and encouraged her to stay true to her artistry. The meeting, which Grande described as “the most encouraging moment of my early career,” helped solidify her confidence in pursuing a solo path.

Mariah Carey's Early Support

Mariah Carey, a longtime mentor to Grande, offered guidance on vocal technique and songwriting. Carey’s willingness to share industry insights during recording sessions in 2011 contributed to the development of Grande’s debut single “The Way.”

Beyoncé's Endorsement

In 2013, Beyoncé publicly praised Grande on social media, calling her “the next big thing.” Beyoncé’s endorsement increased Grande’s visibility and led to collaborative opportunities within the pop and R&B communities.

Impact on Ariana's Career

The combined encouragement from Madonna, Mariah Carey, and Beyoncé provided Ariana Grande with industry validation and a broader network. These early supporters played a pivotal role in her transition from a television actress to a chart‑topping recording artist.
