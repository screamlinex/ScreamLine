Selena Gomez Brings Eighties Luxe and Love to ‘In the Dark’ Video

Summary: The song features on the soundtrack for Season Two of rom‑com series *Nobody Wants This*.

Visuals and Style

Selena Gomez’s “In the Dark” video opens with neon-lit cityscapes and retro 1980s fashion, echoing the aesthetic of classic synth‑wave cinema. Gomez appears in a pastel pink jumpsuit and oversized sunglasses, dancing against a backdrop of animated LED grids and chrome accents. The cinematography emphasizes bold color blocking and slow‑motion sequences that accentuate the track’s dreamy vibe.

Song and Theme

The 3:12‑minute single blends pop sensibilities with a nostalgic synth‑driven hook. Lyrically, it explores the tension between vulnerability and empowerment, with Gomez singing about confronting personal doubts while maintaining confidence. The production, handled by longtime collaborator Stargate, layers airy synth pads with a punchy bassline that keeps the track radio‑ready.

Chart Performance

Since its release on September 12, “In the Dark” has entered the Billboard Hot 100 at number 48, marking Gomez’s 19th top‑50 entry. The track also peaked at number 5 on the Billboard Pop Songs chart and reached the top 10 in Australia and Canada, indicating strong international reception.

Promotion and Release

Gomez debuted the video during a live stream on her Instagram account, where she discussed the inspiration behind the 80s aesthetic. She also performed the track on *The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon* and teased the song’s inclusion in *Nobody Wants This* Season 2 during the series’ promotional trailers. The single is available across all major streaming platforms and was added to Spotify’s “Pop Rising” playlist.
