Chrissy Chlapecka Reconnects With Her Inner ‘Fierce Diva’ in Cyberpop ‘Apollo’ Video

Summary: Chrissy Chlapecka says, “We need queer artists to be as loud, as colorful, and as fierce as possible,” as she drops the cyberpop single “Apollo” and announces her EP Christine, slated for release on Nov. 21.

Cyberpop Video Debut

The new video for “Apollo” premiered on YouTube and features a neon-lit, futuristic set with glitchy visual effects. The clip showcases Chlapecka’s signature high-energy performance style, blending synth‑driven beats with sharp choreography.

Artist’s Vision

In a statement released with the video, Chlapecka explained that “Apollo” is an exploration of self‑affirmation and queer visibility. She emphasized the importance of loud, colorful expression for LGBTQ+ creators.

Upcoming EP

The EP, titled Christine, will include five tracks that combine cyberpop production with introspective lyrics. It is scheduled for digital release on November 21, with a limited edition vinyl to follow.

Industry Response

Early reviews from niche music blogs highlight Chlapecka’s bold production and vocal versatility. Critics note the track’s potential for mainstream crossover while maintaining its queer cultural roots.
