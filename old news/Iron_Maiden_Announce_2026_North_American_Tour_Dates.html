Iron Maiden Announce 2026 North American Tour Dates

Summary: Europa Press via Getty Images

Tour Overview

British heavy‑metal band Iron Maiden has announced a full North American tour for 2026, returning to the continent after a brief hiatus. The tour will support the forthcoming album “Eternal Storm,” slated for release in early 2026.

Dates and Venues

The 2026 tour will span 35 dates across 22 U.S. cities and 3 Canadian venues. Key stops include Los Angeles (Staples Center, March 15), Chicago (United Center, April 3), New York (Madison Square Garden, April 21), and Toronto (Scotiabank Arena, May 5). The schedule will conclude in Seattle (KeyArena, June 10).

Ticket Sales

Tickets are expected to go on sale on March 1, 2026, via the official Iron Maiden website and major ticketing partners such as Ticketmaster and AXS. Early‑bird pricing will be available for the first 48 hours, with standard pricing set at $75–$120 depending on venue capacity.

Band Comments

Lead vocalist Bruce Dickinson expressed enthusiasm: “We’re thrilled to bring our new material to fans across North America. It’s a chance to reconnect with our audience and deliver an unforgettable live experience.”
