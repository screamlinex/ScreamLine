Chilean Filmmakers of Oscar-Winning ‘Bear Story’ Reteam on Feature Debut ‘Brave Cat’; Indie Sales Unveils Teaser (EXCLUSIVE)

Summary: The creative duo behind Chile’s Oscar‑winning animated short *Bear Story*—director Gabriel Osorio and producer Pato Escala—have reunited for their first feature film, *Brave Cat*. The project, a whimsical adventure about a feline hero, has been acquired by Paris‑based sales agent Indie Sales ahead of the upcoming AFM, and a teaser trailer has just been unveiled to generate buzz.

From Oscar to Feature: The Journey of *Brave Cat*

After *Bear Story* swept the Academy Awards in 2021, Chilean filmmakers Gabriel Osorio and Pato Escala set their sights on a larger canvas. *Brave Cat* promises to blend the same heartfelt storytelling and striking animation style that earned them international acclaim, but with a broader narrative scope and a fully realized world. The film is slated for a 2025 theatrical release, with the team hoping to bring a uniquely Chilean perspective to the global animation landscape.

Meet the Creators: Osorio & Escala

Osorio, who directed *Bear Story*, is known for his lyrical visual storytelling and his ability to weave complex emotions into concise narratives. Escala, a seasoned producer with a background in independent cinema, has a knack for turning bold ideas into marketable projects. Together, they’ve cultivated a collaborative environment that balances artistic integrity with commercial viability—a partnership that will be at the heart of *Brave Cat*’s development.

Indie Sales and Distribution Strategy

Paris‑based Indie Sales has taken on the global sales rights for *Brave Cat*, positioning the film for a wide release across key markets. The company’s track record in securing deals for auteur-driven features dovetails perfectly with the creators’ vision. Indie Sales plans to showcase the film at AFM and other major festivals, targeting both theatrical and streaming platforms to maximize audience reach.

Teaser Reveal and First Impressions

The teaser trailer, released on Indie Sales’ social channels, offers a glimpse of the film’s vibrant animation and the central feline protagonist’s daring quest. Early reactions highlight the film’s blend of humor and heartfelt moments, echoing the emotional depth that made *Bear Story* a global hit. The teaser ends with a cryptic tagline—“Every whisker has a story”—hinting at the narrative’s exploration of identity and courage.
