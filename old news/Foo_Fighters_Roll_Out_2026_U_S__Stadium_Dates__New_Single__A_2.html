Foo Fighters Roll Out 2026 U.S. Stadium Dates, New Single ‘Asking for a Friend’

Summary: Since our return to the stage in San Luis Obispo five weeks ago, we have been reminded of why we love and are forever devoted to doing this Foo Fighters thing," Dave Grohl says in a statement.

New Single Release

The Foo Fighters debuted the single “Asking for a Friend” on Tuesday, making it available across all major streaming services. The track blends the band’s classic guitar-driven energy with introspective lyrics, showcasing Grohl’s dynamic vocal performance and the rhythm section’s tight groove.

2026 U.S. Stadium Tour Dates

The band announced a 2026 stadium tour covering 20 major U.S. venues, beginning in Los Angeles on March 12 and wrapping up in New York City on June 15. Ticket sales for the tour will open on April 1, with presale access for fan club members.

Band Statement

In a press release, Grohl emphasized the band’s renewed vigor and commitment to live performance. He noted that returning to large audiences after pandemic restrictions has reignited their passion for touring and connecting with fans worldwide.

Tour Logistics

The 2026 shows will feature a rotating setlist, updated stage visuals, and a 3‑hour show format. The Foo Fighters will partner with local artists for opening acts at select dates, adding a regional flavor to the national tour.
