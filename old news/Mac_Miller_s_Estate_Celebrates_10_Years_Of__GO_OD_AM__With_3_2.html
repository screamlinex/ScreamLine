Mac Miller’s Estate Celebrates 10 Years Of ‘GO:OD AM’ With 3 New Songs

Summary: Mac Miller’s estate marks the 10th anniversary of his debut album *GO:OD AM* with the release of three new tracks and a fresh visualizer for “Rush Hour.”

Album Anniversary Celebration

On October 25, 2025, the estate of late rapper Mac Miller released a commemorative package celebrating a decade since *GO:OD AM* debuted in 2014. The collection includes three previously unreleased songs that were recorded during the original sessions.

New Tracks Released

The added songs—“Midnight Drive,” “Sunset Vibes,” and “Echoes”—feature Miller’s signature smooth flow and introspective lyricism. All three tracks were produced by the same team that worked on the original album, preserving the sonic continuity of the project.

Visualizer for “Rush Hour”

Alongside the new music, a stylized visualizer for “Rush Hour” was unveiled. The animation blends vibrant color gradients with subtle nods to the era’s aesthetic, offering fans a fresh visual experience for a classic track.

Legacy and Impact

*GO:OD AM* has remained a cornerstone of Mac Miller’s discography, influencing a generation of hip‑hop artists. The anniversary release not only honors his legacy but also reintroduces his early work to new audiences.
