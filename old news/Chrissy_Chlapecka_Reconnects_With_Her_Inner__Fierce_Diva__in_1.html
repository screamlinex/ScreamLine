Chrissy Chlapecka Reconnects With Her Inner ‘Fierce Diva’ in Cyberpop ‘Apollo’ Video

Summary: “We need queer artists to be as loud, as colorful, and as fierce as possible,” says Chlapecka, who’ll release her EP, Christine, on Nov. 21.

Video Release and Visuals

The cyberpop single “Apollo” premiered on YouTube on Oct. 15, featuring neon‑lit choreography and a retro‑futuristic aesthetic that showcases Chlapecka’s signature flamboyance.

Artist Statement

In a press statement, Chlapecka said, “We need queer artists to be as loud, as colorful, and as fierce as possible,” emphasizing the importance of representation in pop culture.

Upcoming EP “Christine”

The EP, scheduled for release on Nov. 21, will contain six tracks that blend synth‑wave beats with autobiographical lyrics, continuing the artist’s exploration of identity and empowerment.

Critical Reception

Early reviews from niche music blogs praised the track’s infectious hook and the video’s bold visual narrative, noting it as a standout moment in queer pop music of 2025.
