Movies & TV Shows / October 27, 2025

China Box Office: ‘Row to Win’ Remains No. 1 as ‘Colorful Stage! The Movie’ Opens in Second’

Summary: Over the weekend of Oct. 24–26, “Row to Win” retained the top spot at China’s box office, earning RMB27.6 million ($3.9 million). The PMF Pictures release has now accumulated $51.4 million since its late‑September debut. Meanwhile, “Colorful Stage! The Movie” opened in second place, adding fresh competition to the market.

Row to Win Holds Top Spot

Directed by Ma Lin and headlined by Huang Bo and Fan Chengcheng, “Row to Win” continues its strong run, delivering a solid RMB27.6 million for the weekend. The film’s blend of humor and heart has resonated with audiences nationwide, and its cumulative earnings of $51.4 million underscore its staying power in a crowded release slate.

Colorful Stage! The Movie Debuts Strongly

“Colorful Stage! The Movie” made a splash on opening day, securing the second position behind the blockbuster. While the exact weekend takings are still being tallied, early reports suggest the film is performing well against its peers, signaling a healthy appetite for new Chinese productions.

Industry Context

Artisan Gateway’s latest data highlights a robust domestic market where local comedies and dramas are outperforming many foreign titles. The continued success of “Row to Win” points to a growing trend of audience preference for relatable, locally flavored storytelling, while “Colorful Stage!” demonstrates that fresh, high‑budget productions can quickly capture viewer interest.

Future Outlook

With “Row to Win” firmly entrenched at the top and “Colorful Stage!” gaining momentum, distributors are eyeing the upcoming holiday season for further releases. Analysts predict that the next wave of domestic films will focus on innovative narratives and star power to maintain the upward trajectory of China’s box office.
