Movies & TV Shows / October 27, 2025

China Box Office: ‘Row to Win’ Remains No. 1 as ‘Colorful Stage! The Movie’ Opens in Second’

Summary: At the China box office over Oct. 24–26, “Row to Win” stayed in first place for a second straight weekend, earning RMB27.6 million ($3.9 million) and bringing its total gross to $51.4 million since its late‑September debut. The comedy‑drama, directed by Ma Lin and starring Huang Bo, Fan Chengcheng, and others, continues to dominate the domestic market. Meanwhile, “Colorful Stage! The Movie” opened in second place, signaling strong competition from fresh releases.

Row to Win: A Consistent Hit

The film’s steady performance underscores its mass appeal, with a 12% increase in weekend revenue compared to the previous week. Its blend of humor, heartfelt storytelling, and a star‑packed cast keeps audiences coming back, while the director’s signature storytelling style resonates with both younger viewers and older demographics. With a cumulative gross of $51.4 million, “Row to Win” has become one of the top‑earning domestic releases of the year.

Colorful Stage! The Movie: Strong Second Place

Opening in second place, “Colorful Stage! The Movie” captured RMB18.3 million ($2.6 million) in its first weekend, a respectable showing for a new entry. The film’s vibrant musical numbers and relatable coming‑of‑age plot attracted a broad audience, especially in tier‑two and tier‑three cities. Its performance indicates a healthy appetite for fresh, upbeat content amid a crowded release schedule.

Other Notable Releases

In addition to the top two, the weekend also saw strong showings from “The Last Kingdom: Rise of the Dragon,” which earned RMB12.1 million, and “Silent Echoes,” a thriller that pulled in RMB9.4 million. These titles, along with several smaller indie releases, collectively contributed to a robust domestic market, with total ticket sales exceeding RMB140 million for the weekend.

Market Trends & Industry Insights

The sustained success of “Row to Win” highlights the continued dominance of domestic comedies in China’s box office landscape. Meanwhile, “Colorful Stage! The Movie” demonstrates that musical‑drama hybrids still capture significant market share, especially when paired with strong star power and high‑quality production values. As streaming platforms grow, theatrical releases are increasingly relying on fresh, localized content to draw audiences back to cinemas.
