Florence + the Machine Announce 2026 ‘Everybody Scream’ North American Tour

Summary: Florence + the Machine will headline a 2026 North American tour titled “Everybody Scream,” with opening acts Rachel Chinouriri, Sofia Isella, CMAT, and Mannequin Pussy.

Tour Details

Florence + the Machine’s 2026 “Everybody Scream” tour will span the United States and Canada, featuring the band’s latest material alongside classic hits. The tour is billed as an energetic, arena‑level experience, with a full stage production and an expanded live band lineup.

Opening Acts

Rachel Chinouriri brings a dynamic indie‑pop sound; Sofia Isella offers a raw, punk‑inspired edge; CMAT delivers a fresh country‑pop blend; and Mannequin Pussy adds a high‑octane hardcore punk flair.

Dates & Locations

The tour kicks off on May 10, 2026, in Seattle and concludes on September 22, 2026, in Toronto. A total of 28 shows are scheduled across major cities such as Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Dallas, and Montreal.

Ticketing & Promotions

Tickets will go on sale on March 15, 2026, via the official tour website and major ticketing partners. Prices range from $45 to $210, with VIP packages that include meet‑and‑greet access, exclusive merchandise, and a pre‑show lounge experience.

Artist Statements

Florence Welch said, “I’m thrilled to bring this new set of songs to North America and share the stage with such diverse and talented acts. The energy of the tour will be unlike anything I’ve done before.”
