Mastodon's Bill Kelliher on Nick Johnston's Role in Next Album

Summary: Amy E. Price, Getty Images

Bill Kelliher on Nick Johnston’s Contribution

During a recent interview, Mastodon’s guitarist Bill Kelliher emphasized that Nick Johnston has become a central creative force on the band’s forthcoming album. Kelliher noted that Johnston’s songwriting partnership has evolved from occasional co‑writes to a full‑time collaboration, shaping the band’s sonic direction.

Johnston’s Role and Influence

Johnston is credited with contributing both melodic hooks and complex rhythmic structures. He has also taken an active role in arranging vocal harmonies and refining guitar textures, according to Kelliher. The guitarist highlighted Johnston’s ability to translate the band’s heavier roots into more accessible, yet still progressive, compositions.

Production Process

The recording sessions for the new album have been conducted at the band’s home studio, with producer Nick Raskulinecz overseeing the project. Kelliher mentioned that Johnston’s input has been integral from the initial demo stages through to final mix decisions, ensuring a cohesive sound across the record.

Upcoming Release

Mastodon is targeting a mid‑2025 release window for the album, with a series of single releases scheduled to build anticipation. Kelliher confirmed that promotional activities will include a North American tour and a limited edition vinyl launch.
