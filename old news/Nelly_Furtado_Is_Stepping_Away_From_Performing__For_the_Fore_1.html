Nelly Furtado Is Stepping Away From Performing ‘For the Foreseeable Future’

Summary: Nelly Furtado announced she will step away from performing for the foreseeable future, expressing gratitude for her career and affirming her identity as a songwriter.

Official Announcement

In a statement released on her official website, Furtado said, “I have enjoyed my career immensely, and I still love writing music as I have always seen it as a hobby I was lucky enough to make into a career. I’ll identify as a songwriter forever.” She added that the decision is temporary and not a permanent retirement.

Focus on Songwriting

The singer plans to devote her time to writing new material, collaborating with other artists, and exploring songwriting workshops. She emphasized that her passion for creating music remains strong and that she intends to continue contributing to the industry behind the scenes.

Impact on Upcoming Projects

Furtado’s touring schedule, which included dates in North America and Europe, has been canceled. She confirmed that any pending album releases will proceed as scheduled, but she will no longer perform live during this period.

Fan and Industry Response

Fans expressed support on social media, praising her for prioritizing personal well‑being. Industry peers have welcomed the move, noting her extensive catalog and influence as a songwriter.
