Fenix E35R flashlight review

Summary: A pocketable everyday carry (EDC) flashlight that comes with an IP68 waterproof rating, five lumen modes and is rechargeable using USB‑C.

Design & Build

The Fenix E35R packs a rugged, anodized aluminum body that feels solid yet light at just 85 g. Its compact 4.3‑inch length keeps it comfortably in a pocket or small case. The IP68 rating guarantees protection against dust ingress and submersion up to 1.5 m for 30 minutes, making it suitable for harsh outdoor conditions. A magnetic charging port and a clear LED indicator panel add to the device’s user‑friendly ergonomics.

Performance & Modes

Equipped with a high‑efficiency Cree XP-E2 LED, the E35R delivers up to 3,500 lumens in a single, focused beam. Five modes—Ultra, High, Medium, Low, and Red—allow users to balance brightness with battery life. The beam spread ranges from a tight 1.5° at Ultra to a wide 10° at Low, providing versatility for tasks from headlamps to tactical illumination. The on‑board 1.2 V/1.5 Ah Li‑ion battery sustains 12 minutes at Ultra and 3 hours at Low.

Battery & Charging

The integrated USB‑C port supports 5 W quick‑charge, replenishing the battery to 50 % in just 30 minutes. A dedicated power‑management IC ensures safe charging cycles and protects against over‑discharge. The battery’s cycle life is rated at 500 cycles to 80 % capacity, which aligns well with typical EDC usage patterns.

Use Cases & Verdict

For hikers, the E35R’s IP68 rating and rapid recharge make it a reliable companion. In tactical scenarios, the red‑light mode preserves night vision while maintaining situational awareness. Its small footprint and robust construction render it ideal for everyday carry, whether tucked into a pocket, a backpack, or a keychain. Overall, the Fenix E35R delivers a balanced blend of performance, durability, and convenience—making it a standout choice in the crowded EDC flashlight market.
