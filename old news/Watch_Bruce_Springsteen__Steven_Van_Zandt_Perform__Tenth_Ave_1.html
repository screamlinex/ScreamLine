Watch Bruce Springsteen, Steven Van Zandt Perform ‘Tenth Avenue Freeze-Out’ at the Stone Pony

Summary: Bruce Springsteen and Steven Van Zandt delivered a high‑energy rendition of “Tenth Avenue Freeze‑Out” at the Stone Pony, adding “I Don’t Want to Go Home” to support Van Zandt’s TeachRock nonprofit.

Event Overview

The concert took place on June 15 at the iconic Stone Pony in Asbury Park, New Jersey. The venue, known for launching rock careers, hosted a special showcase featuring Springsteen and Van Zandt in a one‑night collaboration.

Performance Highlights

Springsteen and Van Zandt opened with their signature hit “Tenth Avenue Freeze‑Out,” delivering tight vocal harmonies and a dynamic stage exchange. Midway, they surprised the crowd with “I Don’t Want to Go Home,” a track that resonated with the evening’s philanthropic focus.

Benefit Details

The event served as a benefit for TeachRock, Van Zandt’s nonprofit that provides music education to underserved youth. All proceeds from ticket sales and merchandise were earmarked for scholarships and program funding.

Audience Reaction

The packed venue was filled with longtime fans and new listeners, all of whom cheered enthusiastically. Social media posts highlighted the palpable energy and the artists’ commitment to community outreach.
