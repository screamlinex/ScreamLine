Iron Maiden Will Bring Their 50th Anniversary ‘Run for Your Lives’ Tour to North America in 2026

Summary: Iron Maiden will launch the “Run for Your Lives” 50th Anniversary tour in North America in 2026, with Megadeth and Anthrax serving as special guests. The setlist will feature tracks from the band’s first nine studio albums.

Tour Overview

Iron Maiden’s “Run for Your Lives” tour marks the band’s half‑century milestone. The North American leg will kick off in early 2026, spanning 35 dates across the United States and Canada. The tour will showcase the classic lineup of Bruce Dickinson, Adrian Smith, Dave Murray, Steve Harris, and Nicko McBrain.

Guest Acts

Heavy‑metal veterans Megadeth and Anthrax will join Iron Maiden on select dates as special guests. Both groups will perform their own setlists, adding a rare headlining crossover for fans.

Setlist Composition

Iron Maiden’s set will draw exclusively from the first nine studio albums, spanning “Iron Maiden” (1980) to “Seventh Son of a Seventh Son” (1992). Songs such as “The Trooper,” “Hallowed Be Thy Name,” and “Run to the Hills” are expected to be mainstays.

Key Dates and Venues

The tour will begin in Atlanta, GA, on March 12, 2026, and conclude in Toronto, ON, on May 30, 2026. Major stops include Madison Square Garden (NYC), Staples Center (LA), and Rogers Centre (Toronto).

Ticketing and Production

Tickets will go on sale through major outlets and the Iron Maiden website. The production will feature a 360‑degree stage, LED displays, and a 12‑point drum kit for Nicko McBrain. Fans can expect the classic “Iron Maiden” logo to appear in full‑color on the main screen throughout the show.
