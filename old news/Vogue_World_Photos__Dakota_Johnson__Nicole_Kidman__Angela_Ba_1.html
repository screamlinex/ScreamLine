Vogue World Photos: Dakota Johnson, Nicole Kidman, Angela Bassett as Queen Ramonda from ‘Black Panther’ and More Celeb Pics

Summary: Vogue World seized the Paramount Studios lot in Los Angeles on Sunday night for its “Vogue World: Hollywood 2025” fashion show, a dazzling showcase that blended high fashion with iconic Hollywood costume design. The event highlighted designers like McQueen, Louis Vuitton, Miu Miu, ALAÏA, Marc Jacobs, Valentino, and Balmain, each collaborating with legendary costume designers to reinterpret classic film looks. Among the star‑studded lineup, Dakota Johnson, Nicole Kidman, and Angela Bassett captivated audiences as they stepped into the roles of Queen Ramonda, bringing a fresh, glamorous edge to the beloved ‘Black Panther’ character.

Dakota Johnson’s Reimagined Royalty

Johnson dazzled in a shimmering, emerald‑green gown that echoed the regalia of Wakanda’s monarchy. The designer’s choice of intricate beadwork and a dramatic cape created a striking silhouette that paid homage to the original costume while infusing contemporary couture flair. Photographers captured her poised stance, highlighting the blend of strength and elegance that defines Queen Ramonda’s presence on screen.

Nicole Kidman as Queen Ramonda

Kidman’s rendition of Ramonda was a masterclass in understated power. She wore a minimalist, matte gold dress with a subtle, high‑collared neckline, allowing the focus to remain on her commanding expression. The costume’s sleek lines were complemented by a layered, metallic hairpiece that added a regal sparkle, echoing the character’s dignified aura.

Angela Bassett’s Regal Transformation

Bassett brought a bold, avant‑garde twist to Queen Ramonda’s look. Her ensemble featured a structured, asymmetrical jacket in deep royal blue, paired with a dramatic, floor‑length cape that moved gracefully with each step. The striking use of bold, metallic accessories and a meticulously crafted headpiece underscored Bassett’s iconic commanding presence.

Design Collaboration and Behind‑the‑Scenes Highlights

The event’s success hinged on the seamless collaboration between high‑fashion houses and celebrated costume designers. Each designer brought a unique perspective, blending cinematic history with cutting‑edge runway aesthetics. Behind the scenes, models rehearsed intricate choreography to match the fluidity of the costumes, while designers fine‑tuned details to ensure authenticity and visual impact.
