Hear Mumford & Sons, Hozier Team Up on Rousing Single ‘Rubber Band Man’

Summary: The song follows the rock band's recent album, Rushmere, which dropped in March.

Collaboration Background

Mumford & Sons and Hozier announced the partnership after a series of live performances together during the UK tour earlier this year. The collaboration marks the first joint release between the two acts.

Track Details

“Rubber Band Man” is a mid-tempo folk‑rock track that blends Mumford & Sons’ signature banjo-driven instrumentation with Hozier’s soulful vocal delivery. The lyrics explore resilience and unity in a metaphorical “rubber band” narrative.

Release and Promotion

The single was released on 12 October 2025 as a digital download and streaming single, accompanied by a lyric video on YouTube. Both artists promoted the track on social media platforms and performed a live acoustic session on BBC Radio 2.

Critical Reception

Music critics praised the track for its cohesive blend of styles and the chemistry between the lead vocalists. “Rubber Band Man” has already entered the top 20 of the UK Singles Chart.
