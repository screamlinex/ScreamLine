Sydney Sweeney on Filming ‘Euphoria’ Season 3: ‘Feels Like I’m Going Home to Family’

Summary: Sydney Sweeney is set to wrap up filming “Euphoria” Season 3 in just two days, after a three‑year break from the hit series. Speaking at the AFI Fest premiere of her new drama “Christy,” the actress said returning to the familiar crew feels like coming home to family, and she’s excited to dive back into the world of Rue and her friends.

Back in the Saddle: Returning to Euphoria

After a three‑year hiatus, Sydney Sweeney is re‑joining the “Euphoria” set for Season 3. “I wrapped in two days,” she told reporters, underscoring the whirlwind pace of the shoot. “It’s a relief to be back with the same crew,” she added, highlighting the continuity that keeps the series’ creative energy alive.

AFI Fest Spotlight: “Christy” Premieres

While filming “Euphoria,” Sweeney also took the stage at the AFI Fest premiere of her new drama “Christy.” The event, held in Los Angeles, showcased the actress’s versatility, contrasting her breakout role on HBO with a more grounded, character‑driven performance in the new film.

Family in the Studio: The Crew’s Impact

One of the biggest comforts for Sweeney is the sense of family that the “Euphoria” crew provides. “It’s mostly the same crew, so it feels like I’m going home,” she said. The camaraderie on set, she noted, makes the intense emotional work feel safer and more collaborative.

Looking Ahead: What’s Next for Sweeney?

With “Euphoria” wrapping and “Christy” already in the spotlight, Sweeney’s schedule is shaping up to be a busy one. She hinted at future projects that could further diversify her portfolio, but confirmed that she remains focused on delivering a powerful performance in Season 3’s final chapters.

Personal Reflections on the Journey

Reflecting on the past three years, Sweeney described the experience as both a professional and personal growth period. “Coming back to this show feels like reconnecting with a part of myself that I left behind,” she said. She thanked her fans for their support and expressed excitement for what’s to come.
