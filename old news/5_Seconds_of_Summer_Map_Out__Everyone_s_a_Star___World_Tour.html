5 Seconds of Summer Map Out ‘Everyone’s a Star!’ World Tour

Summary: 5 Seconds of Summer announced a global “Everyone’s a Star!” tour and released the single “Telephone Busy” from their forthcoming album.

Tour Announcement

The Australian pop-rock group confirmed a 2025 world tour titled “Everyone’s a Star!” featuring dates across North America, Europe, Oceania and Asia. The tour will kick off in Los Angeles on March 15 and conclude in Tokyo on June 30.

Tour Details

Each show will include a 90‑minute setlist, with the band performing hits such as “She Looks So Perfect” and “Youngblood.” The tour will also feature a new stage design that incorporates LED visuals and a live DJ set during interludes.

New Single Release

Alongside the tour announcement, 5SOS released “Telephone Busy,” a synth‑driven track that showcases the band’s signature vocal harmonies. The single is available on all major streaming platforms.

Upcoming Album

The new single is the first of several tracks previewed from the group’s upcoming album, slated for release in September. The album will feature collaborations with producer Jack Antonoff and will explore themes of fame and personal growth.

Fan Reactions

Social media buzz highlights excitement for the tour and positive reception of “Telephone Busy.” Fans praise the band’s continued evolution while anticipating the new album’s release.
