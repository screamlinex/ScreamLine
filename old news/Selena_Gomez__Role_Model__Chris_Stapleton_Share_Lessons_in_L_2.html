Selena Gomez, Role Model, Chris Stapleton Share Lessons in Love on ‘Nobody Wants This’ Soundtrack

Summary: Dermot Kennedy, Alessia Cara, Kacey Musgraves, Finneas, Givēon, and more recorded songs for the second season of the popular Netflix series.

Selena Gomez on Love and Inspiration

Gomez, known for her role as a role model, contributed to the soundtrack with a track that emphasizes the importance of self‑love and resilience. She highlighted how her personal experiences informed the songwriting process.

Chris Stapleton’s Take on Relationships

Stapleton brought his signature country‑rock style to the album, offering a raw perspective on heartbreak and healing. He explained how the narrative aligns with the series’ themes of personal growth.

Netflix Series Context

The second season of the series, which has seen a surge in viewership, uses the soundtrack to deepen character arcs and underscore key emotional moments, reinforcing the show’s storytelling.

Featured Artists and Production

Alongside Gomez and Stapleton, artists such as Dermot Kennedy, Alessia Cara, Kacey Musgraves, Finneas, and Givēon recorded tracks that blend pop, folk, and indie influences. Producers coordinated the mix to match the show’s tone and pacing.

Audience Reception and Streaming Impact

Early streaming data indicates the soundtrack has boosted the series’ overall engagement, with several songs climbing the charts within days of release and driving increased viewership for the new season.
