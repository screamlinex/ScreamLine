J-Hope Previews New Concert Film With ‘Killin’ It Girl’ Performance

Summary: Hope on the Stage the Movie arrives in theaters worldwide on Nov. 12 and 15.

J‑Hope Teases New Film

During a recent event, BTS member J‑Hope previewed the upcoming concert film, giving fans a glimpse of his energetic performance of “Killin’ It Girl.” The clip was shared on his official social media accounts and generated immediate buzz.

Film Details

"Hope on the Stage" is a full‑length concert film that captures J‑Hope’s solo tour across Asia. It includes behind‑the‑scenes footage, interviews, and the live show, offering an intimate look at his artistry and stage presence.

Release Schedule

The movie will hit theaters worldwide on November 12, with a second release window on November 15 to accommodate international audiences. Streaming options will follow in the coming months.

Fan Reaction

Early reactions to the “Killin’ It Girl” teaser have been overwhelmingly positive, with fans praising the high‑energy choreography and J‑Hope’s charismatic performance. Social media platforms saw a surge of posts using the hashtag #HopeOnTheStage.
