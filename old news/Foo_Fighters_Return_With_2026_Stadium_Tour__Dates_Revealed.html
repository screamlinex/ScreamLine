Foo Fighters Return With 2026 Stadium Tour, Dates Revealed

Summary: Kevin Winter, Getty Images for iHeartMedia

Tour Overview

The Foo Fighters have announced a 2026 stadium tour that will span North America, Europe, and Asia. The tour marks the band's return to large venues after a hiatus due to the COVID-19 pandemic and a recent world tour that concluded in 2025.

Tour Dates and Locations

Key dates include opening shows in Los Angeles (June 12–14, 2026), a multi-day run in London (August 5–7, 2026), and a headline appearance in Tokyo (September 22, 2026). A full schedule will be released on the band's official website and ticketing partners.

Ticket Information

Tickets will be available through Ticketmaster and Live Nation. General admission and VIP packages are expected to start at $75 and $350, respectively. Early bird pricing will be offered for the first 48 hours after release.

Band Lineup

Frontman Dave Grohl will lead the band, supported by bassist Nate Mendel, drummer Taylor Hawkins (posthumously honored with a tribute segment), and guitarist Chris Shiflett. The show will feature a mix of classic hits and new material from the forthcoming album.
