Korea Box Office: ‘Chainsaw Man The Movie: Reze Arc’ Leads Again

Summary: “Chainsaw Man The Movie: Reze Arc” stayed No. 1 at the Korean box office with $1.6 million from 219,686 admissions over Oct. 24–26, taking a 30.1 % market share on 932 screens. According to KOBIS, the tracking service operated by the Korean Film Council, the film has reached $18.9 million since opening on Sept. 24. The movie’s sustained success underscores the growing appetite for anime‑based thrillers in Korea, as it continues to dominate screens and attract a diverse audience.
