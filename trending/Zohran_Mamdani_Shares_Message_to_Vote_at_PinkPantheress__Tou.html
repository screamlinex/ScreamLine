Zohran Mamdani Shares Message to Vote at PinkPantheress’ Tour Opener: ‘Our Time Has Come’

Summary: While performing “True Romance” at PinkPantheress’ tour opener in Brooklyn, the singer shared a heartfelt message encouraging fans to vote for New York City mayoral candidate Zohran Mamdani, emphasizing that “our time has come.”

Event Details

PinkPantheress launched her U.S. tour on March 15 at Brooklyn’s Brooklyn Bowl. The setlist opened with the hit “True Romance,” drawing a crowd of over 2,000 fans eager for the artist’s signature blend of indie pop and dance beats.

Mamdani’s Message

During the performance, the singer paused to address the audience, reminding them of the upcoming municipal election. “If you love this city, vote for Zohran Mamdani,” the artist urged, adding, “Our time has come, and we can make a difference together.” The message was accompanied by a brief montage of the candidate’s campaign highlights.

Audience Reaction

Fans responded with enthusiastic applause and chants of “Vote! Vote! Vote!” The moment was captured on social media, with several attendees posting videos of the call to action in the venue’s Instagram Stories.

Political Context

Zohran Mamdani is running for New York City mayor as a member of the Democratic Party. His campaign focuses on affordable housing, public transportation, and community policing reforms, aiming to broaden the city’s political base.
