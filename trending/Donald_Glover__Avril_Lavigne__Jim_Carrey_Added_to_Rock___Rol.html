Donald Glover, Avril Lavigne, Jim Carrey Added to Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Lineup

Summary: Donald Glover, Avril Lavigne, and Jim Carrey have joined the lineup for the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame induction ceremony. Additional performers and presenters announced include Mick Fleetwood, Janelle Monáe, and Nancy Wilson.

New Inductees and Performers

Donald Glover, performing under his stage name Childish Gambino, will take the stage to deliver a set of his latest tracks. Avril Lavigne will return to the hall with a high‑energy performance of her most iconic hits. Jim Carrey, the celebrated actor and comedian, will bring his trademark humor to the event with a brief comedic routine.

Additional Presenters

Mick Fleetwood, famed drummer of Fleetwood Mac, will present the induction for one of the Hall’s living artists. Janelle Monáe is slated to present a special award recognizing groundbreaking contributions to music. Nancy Wilson, the lead vocalist of Heart, will present a lifetime achievement award to a pioneering female artist.

Event Details

The ceremony will be held on Saturday, March 18 at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York. The event will be broadcast live on television and streaming platforms, featuring a mix of performances, tribute videos, and acceptance speeches.
