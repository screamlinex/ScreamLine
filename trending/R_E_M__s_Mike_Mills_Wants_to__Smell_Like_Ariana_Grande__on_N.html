R.E.M.’s Mike Mills Wants to ‘Smell Like Ariana Grande’ on New Supergroup Single ‘My Cologne’

Summary: R.E.M. bassist Mike Mills has teamed up with former Hootie & The Blowfish frontman Darius Rucker and Black Crowes drummer Steve Gorman to form the supergroup Howl Owl Howl. Their debut single, “My Cologne,” is a playful ode to a pop‑star fragrance, referencing the familiar name of a contemporary idol.

Formation of Howl Owl Howl

After years of solo projects and collaborations, Mills, Rucker and Gorman announced the new trio in a joint press release. The members cited a shared appreciation for classic rock hooks and a desire to experiment with modern pop sensibilities.

“My Cologne” – Concept and Lyrics

The track centers on the idea of a fragrance that evokes the persona of a current pop icon. The lyrics weave a narrative of scent, fame, and the fleeting nature of celebrity, all wrapped in a breezy, upbeat melody.

Musical Style and Influences

Musically, the song blends Rucker’s country‑rock background, Gorman’s blues‑driven grooves, and Mills’ jangly, melodic bass lines. The result is a radio‑friendly track with a nostalgic nod to 1990s alternative rock while maintaining contemporary production polish.

Release and Promotion

“My Cologne” was released digitally on August 12 through Republic Records, accompanied by a lyric video that showcases playful, perfume‑themed visuals. The band is scheduled for a brief tour of select U.S. venues and an appearance on a late‑night talk show to promote the single.
