Australia vs India: Second T20 international – teams, start, lineups

Summary: Australia and India play the second of their five T20 series in our featured live text commentary stream on Friday.

Match Overview

The second T20 International between the Wallabies and the Men in Blue is set to be a high‑octane showdown. After a 2‑1 series lead for India in the opening game, Australia will be eager to level the series and put the pressure back on the hosts. With both sides boasting deep batting benches and lethal death‑overs bowlers, the contest promises plenty of fireworks.

Start Time & Venue

The match will kick off at 6:00 pm local time (10:00 am GMT) on the iconic Melbourne Cricket Ground. Fans can catch the live commentary stream on our platform, with commentary updates every 20 seconds.

Key Players to Watch

Australia’s David Warner and Mitchell Starc will look to dominate the middle overs, while India’s Rohit Sharma and Jasprit Bumrah will aim to keep the score aggressive and the wickets tight. Expect a thrilling death‑over showdown from both sides’ finishers.
