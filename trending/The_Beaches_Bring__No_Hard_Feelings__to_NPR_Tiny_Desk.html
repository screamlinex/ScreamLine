The Beaches Bring ‘No Hard Feelings’ to NPR Tiny Desk

Summary: The Beaches delivered an intimate NPR Tiny Desk set featuring tracks from their sophomore album, Blame My Ex, alongside the new single “No Hard Feelings.”

Setlist Highlights

The performance opened with “No Hard Feelings,” showcasing the band’s signature blend of surf‑inspired pop and earnest lyricism. Follow‑up songs included fan favorites “Good Night” and “Stuck in a Dream,” all recorded on Blame My Ex.

Lead vocalist Victoria Legrand’s dynamic vocal delivery was complemented by the tight rhythm section, creating a polished yet relaxed atmosphere typical of the Tiny Desk series.

Band Background

The Beaches, formed in 2019, quickly gained attention for their breezy melodies and relatable songwriting. Their debut album, No Hard Feelings, set the stage for a growing fanbase across the U.S. and U.K.

With the release of Blame My Ex, the group has expanded their sonic palette, incorporating more layered instrumentation and introspective themes.

Audience Reception

Viewers praised the band's live arrangements, noting the authenticity of the Tiny Desk setting. Comments highlighted the contrast between the upbeat hooks and the band's emotionally resonant storytelling.

Social media reactions were largely positive, with several fans sharing how the performance felt like a personal conversation.

Future Plans

Following the Tiny Desk appearance, The Beaches will continue touring throughout the fall, with shows scheduled in North America and Europe. The band also hinted at a forthcoming EP later this year.
