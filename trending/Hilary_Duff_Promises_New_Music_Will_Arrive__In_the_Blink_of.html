Hilary Duff Promises New Music Will Arrive ‘In the Blink of an Eye’

Summary: Hilary Duff signed with Atlantic Records in September, marking the first album in a decade.

New Record Deal

Hilary Duff officially joined Atlantic Records on September 12, 2024. The agreement follows her recent single “Sparks” and positions her for a full-length release in 2025.

Album Timeline

Duff said the new album will be “released in the blink of an eye.” Recording sessions began in early October, with production slated to finish by March 2025. The label plans a spring launch, potentially in May.

Artist’s Vision

In an interview with Billboard, Duff described the project as a “return to pop roots” with contemporary production. She highlighted themes of self‑confidence and resilience, drawing from her decade‑long songwriting journey.
