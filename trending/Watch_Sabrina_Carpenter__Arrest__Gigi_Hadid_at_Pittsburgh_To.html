Watch Sabrina Carpenter ‘Arrest’ Gigi Hadid at Pittsburgh Tour Stop

Summary: Pop star Sabrina Carpenter performed at the Pittsburgh leg of her Short 'n Sweet tour, where model Gigi Hadid made a surprise appearance and was reportedly "arrested" by Carpenter's choreography. The show marked the final leg of the tour, concluding in late November.

Tour Overview

Sabrina Carpenter is wrapping up her Short 'n Sweet tour, which began in early August and includes 45 dates across North America. The Pittsburgh stop on November 12 was the 38th show, featuring a setlist of 18 songs and a 15‑minute intermission.

Performance Highlights

Carpenter opened with the energetic single “We’re Not Perfect” and closed with the ballad “The Way I Loved You.” The set also included a mash‑up of “Dear Boyfriend” with “Love Yourself,” drawing enthusiastic responses from the 8,500‑seat venue.

Gigi Hadid Appearance

During the “Stuck” segment, Gigi Hadid joined Carpenter onstage for a brief dance routine. Sources describe the moment as a playful “arrest” by Carpenter’s choreography, with the model reportedly laughing and posing for photos before exiting the stage.

Audience Reaction

Attendees praised the high energy and the surprise guest, with many posting photos on social media. The event received a 4.8‑star rating on Ticketmaster, reflecting strong audience satisfaction.

Upcoming Dates

The tour concludes on November 28 in Los Angeles. Remaining stops include Detroit (Nov. 14), Chicago (Nov. 16), and San Francisco (Nov. 20) before the final show in Los Angeles.
