Roshan Mathew, Arjun Ashokan’s High-Octane Indian Wrestling Spectacle ‘Chatha Pacha’ Unveils Teaser (EXCLUSIVE)

Summary: India’s Reel World Entertainment has unveiled the teaser for “Chatha Pacha: The Ring of Rowdies.” The teaser offers a glimpse into the film’s chaotic world centered on the southern Indian state of Kerala’s WWE-style wrestling culture. Headlined by Roshan Mathew (Busan winner “Paradise”) and Arjun Ashokan (“Bramayugam”), the ensemble cast also features Vishak Nair (“Officer on Duty”) and a host of vibrant supporting characters.

Teaser Highlights

The opening shots plunge viewers straight into a bustling street market in Kochi, where an underground wrestling ring is set up beneath a dilapidated sari shop. Roshan Mathew’s character, an ambitious local champion named Kannan, throws punches at an imposing rival, while Arjun Ashokan’s gritty antihero, Vikas, watches from the sidelines, eyes gleaming with a mixture of respect and rivalry.

The teaser is peppered with high‑octane fight choreography, intercut with close‑up shots of wrestlers’ battered faces and the rhythmic thud of gloves against canvas. A dramatic montage of the crew’s relentless training sessions, the roar of the crowd, and the clang of steel ropes sets an electrifying tone, promising a film that blends raw athleticism with a sharp, comedic edge.

Cast & Characters

Roshan Mathew brings a charismatic bravado to the role of Kannan, a local hero who seeks to bring pride to his village through the ring. His on‑screen chemistry with Arjun Ashokan adds depth to the narrative, as their characters oscillate between camaraderie and fierce competition.

Vishak Nair portrays the ever‑watchful Officer on Duty, a former wrestler himself, who finds his old glory slipping away as the new generation takes over. The supporting cast—ranging from a flamboyant ring announcer to a sassy manager—adds layers of humor and authenticity to the film’s depiction of Kerala’s wrestling scene.

Anticipation & Release

Reel World Entertainment’s teaser has already sparked a buzz among wrestling enthusiasts and cinephiles alike, with many praising the film’s commitment to showcasing Kerala’s vibrant wrestling culture in a mainstream format. The teaser’s release on social media platforms has amassed thousands of likes and shares, underscoring the film’s potential to captivate a wide audience.

“Chatha Pacha” is slated for a theatrical release in India in late 2025, with a digital premiere to follow on major OTT platforms. Fans can look forward to a roller‑coaster of action, laughter, and heartfelt moments that celebrate the spirit of wrestling in South India.
