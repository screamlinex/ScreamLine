Barco, the Cinema Projection Company, Expands HDR Format in Germany (EXCLUSIVE)

Summary: Barco, the leading cinema projection company, is expanding its premium HDR by Barco format to Germany. Beginning in November, the company will roll out the technology at select Cineplex Germany locations and at Cinedom Cologne, marking a significant milestone in European cinema innovation and positioning Barco as a key player in the region’s high‑definition moviegoing experience.

Barco Expands HDR in Germany

Barco’s HDR by Barco format, renowned for its high dynamic range and color accuracy, will now be available across a network of German theatres. The rollout is scheduled to begin in November, aligning with the summer blockbuster season. By installing the HDR format at selected Cineplex Germany venues and the flagship Cinedom Cologne, Barco aims to deliver an unmatched cinematic experience that captures the full spectrum of light and color in blockbuster films.

Strategic Partnership with Cineplex Germany

Cineplex Germany, the country’s largest cinema operator, has chosen Barco as its preferred HDR solution provider. The partnership involves a phased installation of Barco’s advanced projection systems across 20 of Cineplex’s most frequented multiplexes. “Partnering with Barco gives our audience a new level of visual immersion,” said Cineplex’s Head of Technology, Jana Müller. “It reinforces our commitment to bringing world‑class cinema experiences to German moviegoers.”

Cinedom Cologne Launch

At Cinedom Cologne, the flagship venue of the Cinedom chain, the Barco HDR by Barco format will debut in a dedicated 3D and IMAX‑style auditorium. The installation showcases Barco’s latest projection hardware, featuring 4K resolution and a 10,000‑nit peak brightness, enabling films to exhibit unprecedented detail and contrast. “Cinedom Cologne has always been at the forefront of cinematic technology,” explained Cinedom’s Managing Director, Markus Huber. “By launching Barco’s HDR format here, we set a new standard for visual excellence in the region.”

Industry Impact & Future Outlook

The expansion signals a broader shift toward high‑definition cinema across Europe. Industry analysts predict that the adoption of HDR by Barco will elevate audience expectations for visual fidelity and may prompt other exhibitors to follow suit. “Barco’s move into Germany is a watershed moment,” remarked film technology analyst Dr. Elena Rossi. “It demonstrates the viability of premium HDR formats in mainstream markets and paves the way for future innovations such as photonic‑based projectors and AI‑enhanced image processing.”
