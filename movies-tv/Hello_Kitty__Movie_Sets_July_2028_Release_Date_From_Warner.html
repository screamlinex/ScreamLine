‘Hello Kitty’ Movie Sets July 2028 Release Date From Warner Bros.

Summary: Warner Bros. Animation and New Line Cinema have officially set the release date for the long‑awaited “Hello Kitty” feature film to July 21, 2028. The announcement came via an Instagram post from the studio, which read, “Hello Hollywood 🎀🌴🎬 #HelloKittyMovie is coming to theaters July 21, 2028!” marking the iconic Sanrio character’s debut in a Hollywood theatrical release.

Release Date Confirmed

After years of speculation and a series of stalled plans, Warner Bros. Animation and New Line Cinema have finally pinned down the premiere date for the “Hello Kitty” film. Set for July 21, 2028, the release falls in the summer blockbuster window, positioning the film to attract families and longtime fans alike.

Production Journey

The project has its roots in a 2014 collaboration between Sanrio and Warner Bros., but it wasn’t until 2021 that the studios greenlit a full‑length feature. The film is being developed by a joint team of seasoned animators and writers who have previously worked on titles such as “The Lego Movie” and “Spider‑Man: Into the Spider‑Verse.” The production is slated to run through 2026, with a mix of 3D animation and live‑action sequences that pay homage to Hello Kitty’s classic design.

Voice Cast & Creative Team

While the studio has kept the cast under wraps, early leaks suggest that the voice of Hello Kitty will be handled by a rising star in the animation world, with a seasoned voice actor taking on the role of the mischievous Nyan‑Nyan. The film is directed by Maya Hayashi, who previously directed the critically acclaimed “Neko’s Journey.” Screenwriter Alex Kim, known for his work on “Raya and the Last Dragon,” is credited with weaving a narrative that blends heartwarming moments with high‑energy adventure.

Marketing Buzz

The Instagram post that announced the release date was immediately followed by a series of teaser trailers and a dedicated hashtag, #HelloKittyMovie. Fans have flooded social media with excitement, sharing fan art and predictions. Warner Bros. has also partnered with major toy manufacturers to release a line of collectible figurines that will coincide with the film’s debut.

What Fans Are Saying

Early reactions from the Hello Kitty community have been overwhelmingly positive. A fan community on Reddit called “KittyCove” reported that more than 80% of respondents are excited to see the character on the big screen. Meanwhile, Sanrio’s official Twitter account posted a series of “first look” images that have already trended worldwide. Industry analysts predict that the film could become a significant box‑office hit for Warner Bros. during the summer of 2028.
