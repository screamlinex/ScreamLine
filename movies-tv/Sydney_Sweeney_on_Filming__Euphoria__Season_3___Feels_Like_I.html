Sydney Sweeney on Filming ‘Euphoria’ Season 3: ‘Feels Like I’m Going Home to Family’

Summary: Sydney Sweeney is set to wrap up filming for “Euphoria” Season 3 in just two days, after rejoining the beloved HBO series following a three‑year hiatus. She shared the excitement at the AFI Fest premiere of her new drama “Christy” in Los Angeles, noting that the familiar crew makes the return feel almost like coming home.

Wrapping Up Season 3

After a long break from the show, Sweeney’s return has been met with enthusiasm from both fans and colleagues. The final shoot day is slated for Friday, marking the end of a grueling but rewarding production schedule.

Reuniting with the Crew

“It’s mostly the same crew, so it feels like home again,” Sweeney said, reflecting on the comfort of working with familiar faces. The team’s chemistry has been credited with helping her navigate the emotional intensity of her character’s story arc.

Balancing ‘Christy’ and ‘Euphoria’

While filming “Christy,” Sweeney juggled promotional duties with on‑set responsibilities for “Euphoria.” She praised the flexibility of her schedule, allowing her to give both projects the attention they deserve.

Looking Ahead

With the season’s finale approaching, Sweeney is already speculating about future storylines. She hinted at a possible crossover with other HBO shows, sparking fan theories and excitement for what’s next.
