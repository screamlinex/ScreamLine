Sydney Sweeney on Filming ‘Euphoria’ Season 3: ‘Feels Like I’m Going Home to Family’

Summary: Sydney Sweeney announced that she will wrap up filming “Euphoria” Season 3 in just two days, after a three‑year hiatus from the show. She revealed the news during the AFI Fest premiere of her new drama “Christy” in Los Angeles, expressing excitement about rejoining the familiar crew and the emotional sense of “going home to family.”

Wrapping Up Euphoria

“I wrap ‘Euphoria’ in two days,” Sweeney said with a grin, confirming that the final scenes of Season 3 are almost complete. The actress, known for her fearless portrayals of complex characters, is looking forward to sharing the finished product with fans who have been waiting for the series’ next chapter.

Reconnecting with the Crew

After a three‑year break, Sweeney emphasized that the core team remains largely the same, which makes the transition feel almost like a reunion. “It’s mostly the same crew, so it feels like I’m going back to where I belong,” she explained, highlighting the supportive environment that has become a hallmark of the show’s production.

Premiere of “Christy” at AFI Fest

While celebrating the completion of “Euphoria,” Sweeney also took the opportunity to promote her new drama “Christy.” Premiering at the AFI Fest, the film showcases a different side of the actress, blending her signature intensity with a fresh, introspective narrative.

Personal Reflections

Speaking candidly, Sweeney described the emotional weight of returning to a project that has shaped her career. “It feels like I’m going home to family,” she said, underscoring the deep connection she feels with the series’ creative team and her fellow cast members.

Looking Ahead

With “Euphoria” Season 3 nearly finished and “Christy” already making waves, Sweeney’s future looks promising. She hinted at upcoming roles that will push her artistic boundaries further, while expressing gratitude for the opportunities that allow her to explore diverse storytelling avenues.
