Italian-Filipino Coming-of-Age Comedy ‘I Have to F- – – Before the World Ends’ Lands at Tokyo Gap-Financing Market

Summary: Andrea Benjamin Manenti’s debut feature, I Have to Fuck Before the World Ends, follows 16‑year‑old Ren, a Filipino‑Italian teen caught in his mother Mia’s possessive love, as he navigates a coming‑of‑age journey that straddles Rome and Manila. The film blends humor and heart, aiming to secure financing and animation partners at the Tokyo Gap‑Financing Market while showcasing its cross‑cultural appeal and fresh comedic voice.

About the Film

Set against the backdrop of Italy’s historic streets and the vibrant energy of Philippine summer, I Have to Fuck Before the World Ends captures Ren’s internal conflict as he strives for autonomy. The screenplay weaves a tender exploration of identity, family dynamics, and the universal desire to break free from expectations, all wrapped in a light‑hearted, yet poignant, comedic framework.

Director & Cast

Andrea Benjamin Manenti, a rising talent known for her sharp dialogue and keen eye for cultural nuance, helms the project. The cast features emerging Filipino‑Italian actors who bring authenticity and depth to their roles. Ren is portrayed by a charismatic newcomer whose performance balances youthful awkwardness with a growing sense of agency.

Tokyo Gap‑Financing Market Strategy

Targeting the Tokyo Gap‑Financing Market, the film’s team is positioning the project as a high‑return, low‑risk investment with dual‑market appeal. By emphasizing the film’s blend of European and Southeast Asian sensibilities, they aim to attract co‑producers who can facilitate distribution in both regions and beyond.

Funding & Production Goals

The production is seeking a combined budget of $3 million, with a focus on securing animation partners for the film’s stylized visual sequences. Manenti’s proposal outlines a phased financing model, including pre‑sales, equity participation, and potential streaming platform partnerships to ensure a swift path to completion.
